# RunPsy Editorial Plan 2026

## Purpose

This document turns the raw topic list into a working editorial map for RunPsy:
- clear content pillars,
- priority by business and search value,
- stronger angle for each cluster,
- suggested publishing order,
- slug direction for production.

The focus is not "write everything at once", but build a site that grows in layers:
1. first capture high-intent emotional and crisis traffic,
2. then deepen trust with practical evergreen topics,
3. then expand into broader life, family, and identity themes.

## Editorial principles

- Write for a reader in a real situation, not for an abstract audience.
- Prefer "what to do today" over theory for theory's sake.
- Reduce panic and shame.
- Keep psychological language careful and non-diagnostic unless medically necessary.
- Use recognizable scenarios, phrase examples, checklists, and small action steps.
- Build internal linking by clusters, not random tags.

## Priority system

- `P1` - launch priority. Highest search demand, strongest emotional intent, best trust-building topics.
- `P2` - core evergreen library. Broad demand, high usefulness, strengthens topical authority.
- `P3` - expansion topics. Valuable, but better after core coverage is in place.

## Content pillars

1. Relationships and intimacy
2. Personal effectiveness and development
3. Emotional health
4. Family and children
5. Specific topics of the digital era

## Publishing waves

### Wave 1

Goal: capture emotional search demand and prove usefulness fast.

- Why partner pulls away / emotional distance / silence / support / trust / betrayal
- Breakup recovery / toxic partner / self-worth after pain
- Boundaries in couple / phone checks / saying no / emotional over-functioning
- Anxious attachment / avoidant attachment / codependency
- Anxiety / panic / chronic stress / catastrophizing
- Burnout / numbness / overload / guilt around rest

### Wave 2

Goal: deepen authority and build evergreen traffic.

- Habits / procrastination / discipline / focus / learning
- Self-worth / impostor syndrome / inner critic
- Parenting without yelling / teen communication / family conflicts
- Money in couple / career change / identity crisis / purpose

### Wave 3

Goal: widen topical reach and increase time-on-site through adjacent themes.

- Psychosomatics / body / sexuality / body image
- Spirituality / existential loneliness / life after children leave home
- AI and digital life / public pressure / online loneliness
- Family structure complexity / blended families / transgenerational trauma
